The Cost to Buy Wins in the NBA

A Data-Driven Analysis of Offensive vs. Defensive Wins

There is a clear correlation between how good players are and how much they get paid. But as it turns out, salaries reflect mostly only offensive value.

By using a multiple linear regression model on player salary and their Offensive and Defensive Wins Above Replacement (WAR), we can determine the marginal "market price" for an extra win in each category.

The Market Price of a Single Win Produced

$1,901,000 Offensive Win
$469,000 Defensive Win

The market for offensive wins is a premium market, while you can acquire defensive wins at a significant discount.
